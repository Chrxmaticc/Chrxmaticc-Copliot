## This directory is for handling audio inside the website.

What audio will be stored here?

The [Copilot Website](https://chrxmaticc-copliot.vercel.app) will have a radio station and the audio directory will handle music swapping and just mostly to store audio.

## Will this be able to be activated in terminals?

I will add a update mostly that audio can be played in terminal with a command "/audio MONEY SPREAD.wav" where the Copilot can either play any music file, or the stored radio station in [Chrxmaticc Copilot](https://github.com/Chrxmee-Bits/Chrxmaticc-Copliot).
